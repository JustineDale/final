Valentino bags are made with genuine natural Italian leathers that with the right care will last a lifetime. Be sure to keep your bags out of prolonged direct sunlight or extreme heat. If your bag does get wet use a dry cloth to blot the spots as quickly as possible, and then let the bag air dry away from any heat source.

When your bag is not being regularly used we recommend stuffing it with filler so it retains its shape, and to store it in the yellow dust-cover provided with your bag.

If your bag does get soiled, you can clean it with a slightly damp soft cloth with mild bar soap. Be sure to wipe with the grain of the leather. Then wipe again with the damp cloth but without the soap and let the leather air dry completely.
